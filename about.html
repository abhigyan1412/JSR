Our Story

Enterprise IT Infrastructure Partner: Our Story

Founded in 2016, JSR NetSol Private Limited began as a networking and compute reseller in Delhi's Nehru Place technology district.

Over 10 years, we have grown into a full-lifecycle IT infrastructure solutions company — trusted by enterprises, government bodies, healthcare institutions, and financial services firms across India.

We are not a box-mover. Every engagement begins with understanding the customer's business objectives, existing environment, and constraints.

Right-Fit Engineering

Our certified engineers design, deploy, and support solutions that are right-fit — not oversized, not undersized, and never driven by vendor margin.

With authorised partnerships across multiple leading OEMs — including Dell, HP, HPE, SuperMicro, Lenovo, Asus, Juniper, Aruba, Cisco, Ruckus, Cambium, Fortinet, CheckPoint, OpenText, CrowdStrike, D-Link, TP-Link, Sophos, Palo Alto, Schneider, APC, APW — we have the breadth to recommend the best technology for each workload, and the depth to execute and support it.

Core Values

Our Technical Principles as a Cybersecurity System Integrator

Six guiding commitments that drive our client engagement and operational success.

Customer First, Always

Every recommendation is driven by what's right for the customer's environment, budget, and business — not by vendor incentives or margin targets.

Technical Integrity

We will never recommend a solution we haven't validated. Our architects are hands-on practitioners — certifications are a baseline, not a selling point.

Accountable Delivery

We own the outcome, not just the order. Post-deployment performance, SLA adherence, and long-term support are core commitments — not upsells.

Long-Term Relationships

Our 98% customer retention reflects partnerships, not transactions. We invest in understanding your roadmap so infrastructure never becomes a bottleneck.

Continuous Learning

Technology evolves rapidly. Our team maintains active OEM certifications and lab environments to stay current — so our customers don't have to manage that risk.

Transparent Communication

We surface risks, trade-offs, and constraints early. No overpromising — just clear, documented commitments with defined escalation paths when things don't go to plan.
